      About

      Zhipeng Yu is currently a Ph.D. student advised by Yangang Wang at Southeast University.

      His research focus is on learning-based 3D reconstruction with physical constraints.

      Especially the human-centered interaction reconstruction, such as the interaction between people and clothing, objects.
